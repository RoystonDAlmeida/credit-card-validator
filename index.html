Credit Card Validator

1. Visa cards start with a  4  and have  13  or  16  digits.
2. MasterCard cards start with numbers between  51  and  55  and have  16  digits.
3. American Express cards start with  34  or  37  and have  15  digits.
4. Discover cards start with  6  followed by  011  or any number from  500-599  and have  16  digits.
